Ranking Data

Use this page to provide key institutional data. The data is broken down on 7 subject categories. The “Overall” data category is the prime one and it is obligatory to submit. This data is used in RUR RUR Word University Ranking. Other 6 subject areas data are used for relevant RUR Subject Rankings.

Key Notes:

  • Please provide financial data in your national currency, which is: PKR.
  • If your national currency is incorrect, please contact us at: rankings@roundranking.com.
  • All the data except financial shall be provided as FTE (Full Time Equivalents). Please, see more details about "Full Time Equivalent (FTE)" Counting in Help & Support section.
  • The reported year refers to the end of calendar or academic year. For example, 2020 calendar is equal with 2019-2020 academic year .
