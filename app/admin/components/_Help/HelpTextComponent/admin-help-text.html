Number of academic staff

Academic staff (Faculty staff) are counted as FTE number of staff that are employed in academic positions. Please include your university permanent staff and those employed on a long-term contract basement.

Inclusion criteria:

  • lecturer, reader, assistant/associate professor or professor.

Exclusion criteria:

  • technical support staff: laboratory assistants, clinicians, administrative staff, students (including PhD) who are involved in research activity in the framework of academic programs;
  • researchers – e. g. those staff involved solely in research activities, postdoctoral researchers, research assistants;
  • clinical staff (unless they are also employed in academic positions);
  • technical staff that support the general university infrastructure;
  • students of all levels (undergraduate, master’s, PhD and its equivalent);
  • academic staff that are not active such as honorary positions, visiting staff.
Of which are female
The FTE number of “academic staff” (see the above definition) that are female or recognize themselves as female.
Number of International academic staff

The FTE number of “academic staff” (see the above definition) whose primary citizenship is different from the country where your university is located.

Exclusion criteria:

  • Naturalized citizens;
  • Staff with dual citizenship, where one citizenship refers to the country where your university is located.
